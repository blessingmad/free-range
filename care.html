Housing

Provide a safe coop with proper ventilation, nesting boxes, and roosting space. Ensure protection from predators and enough space for chickens to move freely.

Feeding

Allow chickens to forage for insects, seeds, and grass. Supplement with balanced feed, clean water, and grit for digestion.

Health

Regularly check for parasites, provide dust baths, and ensure vaccinations. Clean the coop frequently to avoid disease.
